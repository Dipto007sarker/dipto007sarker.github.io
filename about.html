Ideality and the purpose of the organization

 

Gana Bandhab Songstha (G.B.S) is a Non-Government, Social welfare, Non-political and Non-profitable

organization. The organization aims to alleviate poverty, illiteracy and social injustice from the society

through enlightening the light of education and social awareness. Moreover, stretching hands to handicaps,

widows, Old-Aged person, and poor peoples to develop their lifestyle and bring equity in society. GBS

views all the equal in status and rights regardless their caste, race, religion and social status. The

organization is nationally approved from the Department of Social Welfare, Government of Peoples’

Republic of Bangladesh. The organization is permitted to receive any foreign donation and work in

collaboration with any international Non-Government Organizations (NGOs) in respective fields.
