ABOUT THE ORGANIZATION

Key features of our organization

Ideality

Gana Bandhab Songstha (G.B.S) is a Non-Government, Social welfare, Non-political and Non-profitable organization. The organization aims to alleviate poverty, illiteracy and social injustice from the society through enlightening the light of education and social awareness. Moreover, stretching hands to handicaps, widows, Old-Aged person, and poor peoples to develop their lifestyle and bring equity in society.

Purpose

GBS views all the equal in status and rights regardless their caste, race, religion and social status. The organization is nationally approved from the Department of Social Welfare, Government of Peoples’ Republic of Bangladesh. The organization is permitted to receive any foreign donation and work in collaboration with any international Non-Government Organizations (NGOs) in respective fields.

Aims

1. Poverty Alleviation Project.
2. Education Project.
3. Stipend to Poor Students.
4. Water and Sanitation Project.
5. Prevention of Aids Project.
6. Maternity & Nutrition Project.
7. Family Planning Project.
8. Saving Environment Project.
9. Women Empowerment Project.
10. Rehabilitation Project for Child Labor.
11. Establishing Technical School.
12. Developing Human Resources.
13. Employment for General Peoples.
14. Agricultural Development.
15. Eliminating Dowry System.
16. Awareness and Preventing Human Trafficking.
